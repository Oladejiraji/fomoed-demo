## 8. Aave Flash Loan Volume Hits All-Time High
**Source:** DeFi Llama · **Duration:** 2:55

Aave's flash loan product is having a moment. Daily volume has hit a new all-time high, driven by a surge in arbitrage activity, liquidations, and complex DeFi strategies that rely on borrowing and repaying within a single transaction.

Flash loans, which allow users to borrow any amount of capital with no collateral as long as the loan is repaid in the same block, have become a foundational tool for DeFi power users. They're used by liquidation bots, arbitrage traders, and protocols that need short-term capital to rebalance positions.

The recent spike correlates with increased volatility across crypto markets, which creates more arbitrage opportunities and triggers more liquidations. Aave continues to dominate the flash loan space, capturing the majority of total volume across DeFi.

For the protocol, more flash loan activity means more fees flowing to the treasury, with no credit risk to the underlying lending markets.
